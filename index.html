Qinlong Wang

At Bytedance, he contributes to the Robust LLM Training Infrastructure and is responsible for the engineering optimization (data processing, training and inference) in Seed3D. Before joining Bytedance, he initiated the DLRover and was a core contributor of ElasticDL at AntGroup.
